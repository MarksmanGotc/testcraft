Scale

Normal (1:1) This means materials are entered exactly as they are. For example, if you have 12,500,000 units of a material, you need to enter 12,500,000.

Million Scale (1:1,000,000) In this mode, material amounts are scaled by one million. This means entering 10 will be interpreted as 10,000,000. It’s a convenient way to work with large numbers without typing all the zeros.

The selected scale applies to both Basic and Gear materials.

Ceremonial Targaryen Warlord

Only for Level 1: Level 1 templates will exclusively use Ceremonial Targ items. This is more material-efficient as these items are cheaper to produce.

Use when needed: Ceremonial Targaryen Warlord items can be used at any level when material constraints make it beneficial. This does not force their use, but allows it as needed.

Odds

Odds: There are three tiers. The normal level is always included by default. You can choose to include the lower levels based on your preferences. Odds affect the Season 0 and Ceremonial Targaryen Warlord items, and only apply at levels 15, 20, and 25.
